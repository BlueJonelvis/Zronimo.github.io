About Me

Welcome all Magnificent Dreamers to the world of Zronimo. Zronimo is an award winning multi talented artist, who aspires to entertain the world with his creativity. Since the age of 13, he has been performing even while going to school. As an adult, he now embraces his abilities to inspire, and draw in large crowds with his talents & hit songs. He is undoubtedly heavily influenced by musical legends and artists such as: Michael Jackson, Prince, and James Brown (which is evident in his music). With the dynamics, dance moves, musicality, soul, and work ethic of his idols, Zronimo seeks to prove that, without a shadow of a doubt, he has what it takes to carve himself into a true Icon.
